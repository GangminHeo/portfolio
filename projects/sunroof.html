Kinetic typography - sunroof

Project Type - Kinetic typography - Motion design

Used Tool - After Effects / Illustrator

Overview

Kinetic typography is a type of motion design, creating an animation based on a text. It is used for various purposes, but one thing that really got me into is a lyrics video. The fascinating flow of motion graphics with the rhythm and groove of the song captivated me hard. And that is going to be my direction to go.

I chose the song “Sunroof” by Nicky Your and dazy. It was one of my most-played songs this year. I set my final goal of creating kinetic typography for a whole song but I had to change it to highlight-only as the project went on. It was pretty challenging since I had to put a lot of time and effort into teaching After Effects to myself as well as creating the motion graphic.

Starting the project, I did some research and set the plan. First, I did research on the song (Sunroof) and tried to understand the overall mood and meaning of the song. The other parts of research was basically just watching a lot of kinetic typography videos on youtube.

I could not build many ideation before I jump onto motion animation and see how they works myself because it is quite hard to sketch those movements inside my head to paper or Illustrator artboard. So I just jumped in After Effects. But last time I used After Effects was 2 years ago and I barely remembered it. I had to go though tons of youtube videos, and online articles that covers from After effects tutorial to some advanced skills.

I almost had nothing completed until mid-semester and all I did was watching a videos on youtube and getting familiar with After Effects. But once I found a interesting technique, I tried to use them in my project. Or sometimes I also came up with a motion idea but I had no clue how to implement that in After effects, and I look up some skills and techniques that help me to bring my ideas to motion graphic. So that is how I built most of my project.
