AR Project - 3D Cake

Project Type - AR, 3D Modeling

Used Tool - Meta Spark Studio / Adobe Substance 3D stager

Overview

I was assigned to create a motion design package, MOGRT, for a event. I chose Worlds 2022 as my event. Worlds 2022 is League of Legends 2022 Worlds Championship is the biggest worldwide LOL competition. It is one of the most enormous and noted Esports league where 24 best teams all around the world fight for the first place. The matches are held in several big stadiums in North America and there are many events such as opening ceremony or player interview.

After I created those assets in After effects, I exported them to premiere pro as mogrt fils and use them for showcasing video.
